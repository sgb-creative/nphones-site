About

One device. Many identities. Zero compromise.

For decades the mobile industry forced a choice: one identity per device, or carry two phones. NPhones eliminates that trade-off with a cloud business phone any enterprise, operator or government can deploy.

Our vision

The second phone disappears

Identity, not hardware

Just as the cloud redefined computing, NPhones decouples the phone from the device — turning every device into many.

Carrier-grade scale

Designed from day one to run on telecom infrastructure worldwide, bringing secure business phones to millions of users.

Security as a default

Isolation, compliance and zero-trust built into the platform’s fabric — not bolted on.
