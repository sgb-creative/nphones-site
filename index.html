The platform

Not another work profile. A complete phone.

NPhones is not an app folder, a dual SIM or an MDM policy. It is an entire business phone running in the cloud — your employees reach it from the device in their pocket.

A whole phone, zero hardware

Dedicated number, WhatsApp Business, email, CRM, contacts and files — everything a business phone carries, delivered from the cloud in minutes instead of shipped in boxes.

Total separation, both directions

Company data never touches the personal phone — only an encrypted display stream reaches the screen. And the company never sees the employee's personal life. Two phones, one device.

The company owns the asset

Numbers, customer chats and contacts stay with the business when people move on. Offboarding is one click — not a chase for a device and a number that left with the employee.

How it works

From console to pocket in minutes

IT replaces phone logistics — boxes, SIMs, shipping, returns — with one cloud console.

01

Create the phone

From the NPhones console, IT creates a cloud phone: number, apps, working hours and policies.

02

Send the invite

The employee gets an SMS with a secure install link. No box to ship, no SIM to activate.

03

Sign in, done

They install the app, sign in with the credentials IT provided, and the business phone is live.

04

Work, separated

Calls, WhatsApp Business, email and enterprise apps run on the business number — fully apart from personal life.
