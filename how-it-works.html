How it works

From console to pocket in minutes.

IT replaces phone logistics — boxes, SIMs, shipping, returns — with one cloud console.

01 · Create the phone

From the NPhones console, IT creates a cloud phone: number, apps, working hours and policies. Templates make the tenth phone faster than the first.

02 · Send the invite

The employee gets an SMS with a secure install link. No box to ship, no SIM to activate, no waiting.

03 · Sign in, done

They install a lightweight app — or use the browser — and sign in. The business phone is live in about 30 seconds.

04 · Work, separated

Calls, WhatsApp Business, email and enterprise apps run on the business number, fully apart from personal life.

Lifecycle

The whole employee lifecycle, handled

Onboarding
